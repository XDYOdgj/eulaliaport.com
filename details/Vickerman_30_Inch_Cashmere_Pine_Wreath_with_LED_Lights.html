Elegant Christmas Wreath for Festive Vibes

This 30-inch Vickerman Cashmere Pine Artificial Christmas Wreath is the perfect way to add a touch of cozy elegance to your holiday decor. Featuring a lifelike cashmere pine design, it creates an authentic, festive look that brings the spirit of Christmas indoors with minimal effort.

Its prelit 50 warm white Dura-lit LED lights are designed to illuminate your space with a soft, inviting glow that lasts all season long. These lights are energy-efficient and powered by microchip technology, which ensures they stay on even if a bulb is missing or broken, eliminating the need for frequent replacements.
