Elevate Your Art and Decor with Professional LED Crystal Display Bases

Whether you're showcasing crystal energy grids, 3D glass art, or acrylic creations, these LED bases add a modern and elegant touch to your displays. With six dynamic lighting modes, you can choose from static or flashing colors to create the perfect ambiance for any space.

The dual power options—either 3 AAA batteries or a USB connection—ensure that your display stays illuminated for up to 20+ hours. This versatility means you can use the bases both indoors and outdoors for flexible lighting needs.

These ultra-compact bases are designed for portability, fitting easily on shelves, desks, or retail displays. The reinforced ABS construction is both durable and lightweight, at only 7oz, and the non-slip pads securely hold your crystals in place.

Experience 360° brilliant illumination with a clean, shadow-free light diffusion, offering a museum-quality lighting solution. These bases are not only functional but also visually striking, making them an excellent choice for enhancing your home decor or artistic displays.

Perfect as a gift for special occasions like Valentine’s Day or weddings, these LED crystal display bases are ideal for bringing out the best in your collection and adding a professional aesthetic to your creative space.
